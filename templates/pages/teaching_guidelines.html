Teaching Guidelines

Become an Instructor on LearnHub

Welcome to LearnHub! We're excited that you're interested in sharing your knowledge with our community. This guide will help you understand how to create engaging courses and become a successful instructor.

Getting Started

  • 1 Create your instructor profile with a professional photo and bio
  • 2 Submit your course proposal for review
  • 3 Once approved, start creating your course content
  • 4 Publish your course and start teaching!

Course Content Requirements

  • Video quality: Minimum 720p HD resolution
  • Audio: Clear audio without background noise
  • Content: Original and educational
  • Structure: Clear learning objectives
  • Each course must have at least 5 lessons

Best Practices

  • Engage students - Use questions, examples, and real-world scenarios
  • Keep it organized - Break content into modules and lessons
  • Be consistent - Maintain a steady upload schedule
  • Respond to feedback - Listen to student reviews and improve
  • Add resources - Provide downloadable materials and exercises

Revenue Share

Instructors earn 70% of the course revenue, paid monthly via bank transfer or PayPal. Free courses help build your reputation and attract students to your paid courses.

Quality Standards

All courses must meet our quality standards. We reserve the right to unpublish courses that:

  • Contain misleading or false information
  • Violate copyright or intellectual property rights
  • Contain inappropriate or offensive content
  • Are poorly produced or lack educational value

Support

If you have any questions or need help, our instructor support team is here to help. Contact us at instructors@learnhub.com
